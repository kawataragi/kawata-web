Anime: Tokyo Revengers

Tokyo Revengers is a Japanese series written by Ken Wakui. It was released in 2017 from manga to anime. The story follows Takemichi Hanagaki, a young man who discovers that his ex-girlfriend, Hinata Tachibana, and her younger brother, Naoto, are killed by a Tokyo gang known as the Tokyo Manji Gang. After an accident that sends him back in time to his middle school days, Takemichi gets the chance to try to change the future and prevent the tragic events from happening.

About Tokyo Revengers

Tokyo Revengers is a compelling series with a mix of gang conflicts, time travel, and character growth. It has gained popularity for its engaging plot and well-developed characters.
